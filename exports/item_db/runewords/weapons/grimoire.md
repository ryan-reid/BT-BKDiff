# Runewords for Grimoire

### Vigilance
* **Runes:** Dol Rune + Gul Rune
* **Base Items:** grim
* **Properties:**
    * 5-10% Chance to cast level 5-10 Ring of Fire when struck
    * +10% Faster Run/Walk
    * +30% Faster Block Rate
    * +20-40 to Life
    * +20-40 to Mana
    * All Resistances +25-35
    * +75-100% Enhanced Defense
