# Runewords for Hand to Hand

### Chaos
* **Runes:** Fal Rune + Ohm Rune + Um Rune
* **Base Items:** h2h
* **Properties:**
    * +15 Life after each Demon Kill
    * +240-290% Enhanced Damage
    * Adds 216-471-216-471 Magic Damage
    * +5 to Whirlwind
    * +35-40% Increased Attack Speed
    * 25-26% Chance to cast level 25-26 Frozen Orb when struck
    * Repairs 1 durability in  seconds

### Pattern
* **Runes:** Tal Rune + Ort Rune + Thul Rune
* **Base Items:** h2h
* **Properties:**
    * +15% Increased Attack Speed
    * +80-120% Enhanced Damage
    * Adds 100-100 Fire/Lightning/Cold Damage
    * All Resistances +15
    * +15 to Strength
    * +15 to Dexterity
    * +1 to Martial Arts Skills
