# Unique Circlet

### Griffon's Eye (Griffon's Eye)
* **Base Item:** Diadem
* **Level Requirement:** 76
* **Properties:**
    * +100-200 Defense
    * +25% Faster Cast Rate
    * +1 to All Skills
    * +10-15% to Lightning Skill Damage
    * -15-20% to Enemy Lightning Resistance

### Kira's Guardian (Kira's Guardian)
* **Base Item:** Tiara
* **Level Requirement:** 84
* **Properties:**
    * +50-120 Defense
    * Fire Resist +50-70%
    * Cannot Be Frozen
    * +20% Faster Hit Recovery
    * +0-6 to Amazon Skill Levels
    * -10-20% to Enemy Fire Resistance
    * +1-5% to Maximum Fire Resist

### King's Absolution (King's Absolution)
* **Base Item:** Coronet
* **Level Requirement:** 90
* **Properties:**
    * +1 to All Skills
    * +0-6 to Amazon Skill Levels
    * 20% Chance to cast Level 1 Lower Resist when you Kill an Enemy
    * +10-15 to Mana after each Kill
    * +10-15 Life after each Kill
    * +5-10% to Experience Gained
    * +66-99 Defense
