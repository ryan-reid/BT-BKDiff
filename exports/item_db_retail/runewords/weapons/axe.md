# Axe Runewords

### Beast
* **Runes:** Ber Rune + Tir Rune + Um Rune + Mal Rune + Lum Rune
* **Base Items:** Axe
* **Properties:**
    * \+40% Increased Attack Speed
    * Level 9 Fanaticism Aura When Equipped
    * \+240\-270% Enhanced Damage
    * \+25\-40 to Strength
    * Level 5 Summon Grizzly \(13/\# Charges\)
    * \+3 to Werebear
    * \+3 to Lycanthropy

### Crescent Moon
* **Runes:** Shael Rune + Um Rune + Tir Rune
* **Base Items:** Axe
* **Properties:**
    * \-35% to Enemy Lightning Resistance
    * Ignore Target's Defense
    * \+180\-220% Enhanced Damage
    * Magic Absorb 9\-11%
    * Level 30 Summon Spirit Wolf \(18/\# Charges\)
    * 7% Chance to cast level 13 Static Field on striking
    * 10% Chance to cast level 17 Chain Lightning on striking

### Doom
* **Runes:** Hel Rune + Ohm Rune + Um Rune + Lo Rune + Cham Rune
* **Base Items:** Axe
* **Properties:**
    * \+280\-320% Enhanced Damage
    * Level 12 Holy Freeze Aura When Equipped
    * \+45% Increased Attack Speed
    * Prevent Monster Heal
    * \-40\-60% to Enemy Cold Resistance
    * \+2 to All Skills
    * 5% Chance to cast level 18 Volcano on striking

### Famine
* **Runes:** Fal Rune + Ohm Rune + Ort Rune + Jah Rune
* **Base Items:** Axe
* **Properties:**
    * \+270\-320% Enhanced Damage
    * 12% Life stolen per hit
    * \+30% Increased Attack Speed
    * Prevent Monster Heal
    * Adds 180\-200 Magic Damage
    * Adds 50\-200 Fire/Lightning/Cold Damage
    * Ethereal
