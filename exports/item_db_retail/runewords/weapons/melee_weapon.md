# Melee Weapon Runewords

* **Runes:** Amn Rune + Ber Rune + Ist Rune + Sol Rune + Sur Rune
* **Base Items:** Melee Weapon
* **Properties:**

    * Slows Target by 33%
    * Level 88 Revive \(8/\# Charges\)
