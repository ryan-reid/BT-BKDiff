# Hand to Hand Runewords

### Chaos
* **Runes:** Fal Rune + Ohm Rune + Um Rune
* **Base Items:** Hand to Hand
* **Properties:**
    * \+15 Life after each Demon Kill
    * \+240\-290% Enhanced Damage
    * Adds 216\-471 Magic Damage
    * \+1 to Whirlwind
    * \+35% Increased Attack Speed
    * 9% Chance to cast level 11 Frozen Orb on striking
    * 11% Chance to cast level 9 Charged Bolt on striking

### Pattern
* **Runes:** Tal Rune + Ort Rune + Thul Rune
* **Base Items:** Hand to Hand
* **Properties:**
    * 10% Bonus to Attack Rating
    * \+40\-80% Enhanced Damage
    * Adds 12\-32 Fire Damage
    * All Resistances \+15
    * \+6 to Strength
    * \+6 to Dexterity
    * \+30% Faster Block Rate

### Mosaic
* **Runes:** Mal Rune + Gul Rune + Amn Rune
* **Base Items:** Hand to Hand
* **Properties:**
    * \+2 to Martial Arts Skills
    * \+50% chance for finishing moves to not consume charges
    * \+20% Increased Attack Speed
    * \+200\-250% Enhanced Damage
    * \+8\-15% to Fire Skill Damage
    * \+8\-15% to Cold Skill Damage
    * \+8\-15% to Lightning Skill Damage
